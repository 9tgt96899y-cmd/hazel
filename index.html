To simulate the language shock of transnational students, the traditional phonetic symbols (Zhuyin) on the blackboard will appear flipped, distorted, or completely mirrored. Can you decipher them?

🎯 遊戲反思 / Reflection:

剛剛那些刻意顛倒、打反、變形的注音符號，是否讓你感到困惑與挫折？

你只困惑了短短的 1 分鐘，但對於許多剛踏入台灣校園的跨國銜轉生而言，這種「聽得懂聲音，卻看不懂黑板文字」的無助與孤立感，是他們每天在課堂上面對的真實寫照，往往長達數個月甚至數年。

Did those intentionally mirrored, flipped, and distorted phonetic symbols frustrate you?

You only experienced this confusion for 1 minute. However, for many transnational students entering Taiwanese classrooms, this feeling of "hearing the sounds but seeing only alien codes on the blackboard" is a harsh daily reality that lasts for months or even years.
